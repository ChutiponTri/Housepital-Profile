## Overview

On **July 17, 2025**, Housepital Care proudly won **1st Prize** at the **Pitch for Change competition**, part of **Aging Innovation Day**, hosted by **YoungHappy, ThaiHealth, and SCBX Next Tech**.

## Key Highlights

- **National Recognition**: Selected as the top winner among **300+ teams nationwide**.  
- **Focus on Aging Society**: Demonstrated impactful solutions tailored to the challenges of an aging population.  
- **Mission Reinforced**: Strengthened Housepital Care’s commitment to transforming healthcare innovation at scale.

## Outcome

This award validated the **dedication and vision** of the Housepital Care team, highlighting its role as a leading force in developing healthcare solutions for Thailand’s rapidly aging society.  
It marks an important milestone in our journey toward **scalable, meaningful healthcare innovation** nationwide.
