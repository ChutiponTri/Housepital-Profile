## Overview

Housepital Care proudly took part in **Startup Thailand 2025: CONNEXT MedTech-HealthTech** on **May 22, 2025**, at Club Siam Glowfish.  
The event featured a dynamic **business matching and networking session**, connecting startups, investors, and industry leaders in the MedTech and HealthTech sectors.

## Key Highlights

- **Collaborative Networking**: Engaged with startups, investors, and healthcare innovators to explore partnership opportunities.  
- **Business Matching**: Showcased Housepital Care’s vision while seeking strategic collaborations to enhance healthcare solutions.  
- **Innovation Focus**: Positioned as part of the broader effort to drive **healthcare innovation in Thailand**.

## Outcome

Participation in this event strengthened Housepital Care’s presence in the **MedTech and HealthTech ecosystem**, opening pathways for future collaboration, investment, and innovation.  
This milestone reinforced the company’s mission to improve healthcare accessibility and efficiency through technology-driven partnerships.
