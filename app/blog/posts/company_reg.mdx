## Company Registration

We are proud to announce that **HOUSEPITALCARE Co., Ltd.** is officially registered as a legal entity. This marks a significant milestone in the development of our innovative **smart home healthcare platform**.

Our company registration will enable us to expand our operations, secure partnerships, and build the foundation for future growth. With this step, we are positioned to provide innovative solutions for remote patient monitoring, telemedicine, and IoT-based health tracking, all while adhering to the highest standards of healthcare innovation.

We are excited about the future and the opportunity to transform healthcare at home, ensuring patients receive quality care no matter where they are.
