## Overview

This phase marked the official registration of Housepital Care as a legal entity, enabling it to operate formally within the med-tech and healthcare innovation space. Establishing the company was a critical first step in unlocking structured fundraising, entering contracts, and scaling the business legally and sustainably.

## Key Features

- **Legal Incorporation**: Registered Housepital Care as a corporate entity, laying the legal groundwork for operations, partnerships, and intellectual property protection.
- **Operational Infrastructure**: Set up official bank accounts, accounting systems, and compliance frameworks to support future product development and deployment.
- **Stakeholder Readiness**: Formalized founding team roles, equity structure, and shareholder agreements, enabling future investor engagement and transparent governance.

## Challenges and Learnings

Navigating legal documentation, aligning founder visions, and ensuring regulatory compliance were key hurdles during the registration process. We learned the importance of legal clarity, early financial structuring, and seeking expert advice in corporate governance from the start.

## Outcome

Housepital Care’s registration established a strong legal and operational foundation, allowing us to approach strategic partners, apply for grants, and begin early-stage deployment with institutional stakeholders. This milestone represents a critical turning point from concept to company.

---

This achievement validates Housepital Care’s long-term vision and opens the door to sustainable growth, market expansion, and deeper engagement across Southeast Asia’s aging care ecosystem.
